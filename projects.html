Lloyd Expressway Traffic Signal Optimization

I conducted research on how changing the logic on which traffic signals on the Lloyd Expressway operate affects traffic efficiency. I used data collected by the state of Indiana to determine traffic flow patterns in conjunction with Simulation of Urban Mobility (SUMO) to simulate traffic flow with varying traffic signal models. Using models created and tested by different researchers, I was able to apply them to a custom road network that reflects the Lloyd Expressway. I submitted this paper to Collegeboard for AP Research, and to the International Baccalaureate Program as my Extended Essay.
