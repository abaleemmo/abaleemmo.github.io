Education

Signature School

2022 fall - 2026 spring

Signature School is a charter school based in Evansville, Indiana. "The mission of the Signature School is to meet the needs of self-motivated learners in a progressive environment driven by global concerns. [They] emphasize rigor and excellence in academics, the arts, integrated technologies, and community service." (Signature.edu)

My time at Signature School was fundamental to my personal and professional growth. With the lowest-level courses being honors classes, I quickly learned how to manage a demanding workload and use my time effectively. The rigor was especially challenging during my sophomore year, when I juggled 10 classes within an 8-period schedule by choosing to take two AP Economics courses online. Over the course of high school, I completed 18 AP and IB classes and a total of 28 courses while actively participating in various clubs. Despite the heavy load, I maintained a 4.59 weighted GPA. Additionally, based on my PSAT score, I will be recognized at a minimum as a National Merit Commended Scholar. I also recieved the highest AP Scholar Award with Distinction from Collegeboard.
